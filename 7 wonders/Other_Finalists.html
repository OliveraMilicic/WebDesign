Wonders of the Ancient World

There is also a separate list, the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, which includes structures from antiquity. These were: the Great Pyramid of Giza, the Hanging Gardens of Babylon, the Statue of Zeus at Olympia, the Temple of Artemis at Ephesus, the Mausoleum at Halicarnassus, the Colossus of Rhodes, and the Lighthouse of Alexandria. Of these, only the Great Pyramid of Giza still stands.


➤ History

The Seven Wonders of the Ancient World represent some of the most remarkable architectural and artistic achievements of antiquity. Created as a kind of travel guide for ancient Greek tourists, the list included monuments that showcased the creativity, engineering skill, and cultural richness of ancient civilizations. Among them were the majestic Great Pyramid of Giza, the legendary Hanging Gardens of Babylon, and the towering Lighthouse of Alexandria. Though only the Great Pyramid still stands today, the legacy of these wonders continues to inspire awe and admiration, reminding us of humanity’s timeless quest to build, imagine, and leave a mark on history.
